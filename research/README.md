# AI Governance Research Findings

This directory contains comprehensive research on global AI governance frameworks, compliance regulations, and regional approaches. The goal is to provide a foundational understanding for building an AI agent governance system.

## Contents

*   **Global Frameworks:** Detailed analysis of international AI governance initiatives like the EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF.
*   **Regional Focus:** Examination of AI regulatory landscapes in key regions such as APAC (China, South Korea, Japan).
*   **Compliance Guides:** Practical guidance and checklists for navigating AI compliance.
